Frequently asked questions

How is this different from other Japanese learning apps?

Unlike traditional flashcard apps, Kanji Battler uses gamification and AI to create an engaging, personalized experience. Each battle adapts to your skill level, and the competitive element keeps you motivated to continue learning daily.

Do I need any Japanese knowledge to start?

Not at all! Kanji Battler starts from absolute zero, teaching you stroke order, meanings, and readings as you play. The AI ensures you're never overwhelmed.

How much time do I need to commit daily?

Just 3-5 minutes a day is enough to see progress. Most users play 2-3 quick battles during breaks. The key is consistency, not long study sessions.

Can I use this to prepare for JLPT exams?

Absolutely! Our curriculum is aligned with JLPT levels N5 through N1. You can filter battles by JLPT level and track your readiness for each exam.

What happens if I miss a day?

No problem! Your progress is saved, and the AI adjusts your next session based on the time gap. We encourage streaks but understand life happens.
